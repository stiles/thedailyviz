I was away from Seoul for much of the 2016 summer, but when I returned it was impossible not to hear all the complaining about the heat.

The summer had indeed been hotter than usual, especially in August, when the daily low temperature a few days actually exceeded the average high.

So obviously I had to document this fact with a chart.
