In recognition of the 50th anniversary of the Zone Improvement Plan, the network of ZIP codes we use for everything from mail delivery to credit card security, I examined the list of more than 40,000 such boundaries and their demographics. Then I wrote about nine worth noting, based on data compiled by the U.S. Census Bureau and Esri, a leading geographic software company.
